Controls:

Movements: Arrow Keys
Jump: Spacebar
Pause: P
Skip Frame: O (When Paused)
Skip Level: L
Render Spatial Debug: S

If you press a button not listed here and some weird stuff happens, then press it again and then never again!
If you do not remember what button you pressed, then reload the page and think about what you've done!

Level One:

You can run around, collect coins, avoid/kill/getkilledby baddies and of course you can fall to your death.
You'll start with three lives but when they have depleted the page reloads and you have to start the whole mess again.
